History of ABN-Team

Predecessor

The predecessor of ABN is a small group of players created by Knight and Simple that are active on the MINECRAFT server "暮桥时光". There are not many people, only 4 ~ 5 people. At this time, ABN did not have an official name, and can only called a "squad".

Expansion

In the early stage of the development of the team, the team began to expand. For this purpose, a special department was established. In fact, the situation remained until YDJSIR and Hoshino Sang (KickKick) joined.

The Join of Donghang

YDJSIR led an independent technology company Donghang to join ABN-Team. After management negotiations, Donghang decided to join the team in the form of alliance and provide technical support for the team. At the same time, there are 1-2 small-scale technology associations joining, and the team size has rapidly increased to more than 30 people. For this reason, the management established a senate to manage each association.

Development of the Senate

With the start of the server plan, the senate's responsibility can no longer be limited to the daily management of the team members; the daily maintenance of the server, the collection of opinions, and the organization and mobilization of various communities all require the senate to go from behind the scenes to the front stage. The development of the Senate provides a good foundation for the successful completion of the server plan and the future expansion of server functions.

Dicatus's Expection

In the survival plan, Dicatus assumed the role of architect for several server cycles. During this period, Dicatus has been admonishing himself that beautiful things don't have to be big and majestic, but leave something touching. What can give passion under the scorching sun and give warmth in the severe winter is beauty. With the development of the server plan, new members will inevitably join, injecting new vitality into the team and bringing new momentum.

The Transition

The first is the event that YDJSIR card ascended the throne (DJ heika, banzai!), Then the communities formally merged into ABN-Team, the organization has also been streamlined, the establishment of ABNAA also makes ABN's technological strength become strong (2333333333333), and ABNteam successfully transformed from a game group to a technology to association, and the development of the server plan also provided help for the future learning and scientific research life of the team members.
